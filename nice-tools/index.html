1.网页截图插件：GoFullPage - Full Page Screen Capture

截图后还可以编辑，例如裁剪，绘图

The simplest way to take a full page screenshot of your current browser window. Click on the extension icon (or press Alt+Shift+P), watch the extension capture each part of the page, and be transported to a new tab of your screenshot where you can download it as an image or PDF or even just drag it to your desktop. 👉 视频演示
